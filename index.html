FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Carfective?
With Carfective, buying a car is simple and stress-free. We provide personalized guidance, insider expertise, and full advocacy -ensuring you choose the right vehicle, understand every detail, and get the best deal. For individuals and businesses, we work solely for you, not the dealerships.
Can I custom build a car with Carfective?
Absolutely. If you have specific requirements, we can first check whether an existing option is already available. If not, we can submit a build request on your behalf, provided the manufacturer offers custom build options. Please note that some manufacturers support custom builds, while others do not.
Where can I get my vehicle serviced after buying through Carfective?
Any dealership for that vehicle brand or your personal / local mechanic of your choice.
What forms of payment does Carfective accept?
We accept Zelle payments.
How do I get my car?
Once you’ve submitted the credit application and have been approved, you will have the option to pick up your vehicle at the dealership or Carfective can deliver it to your home for an additional fee.
Am I buying the car from Carfective or the dealership?
Dealership, all documents will be between the purchaser and the dealership
Can Carfective handle lease returns or trade ins?
Yes. Carfective can assist with lease returns and help with vehicle trade-ins.

For details specific to your situation, let’s discuss and tailor the next steps to your needs.
Can I buy a car anywhere in the U.S. with Carfective?
Yes! We have relationships with dealers across the U.S. and can’t wait to help you!

Additional FAQ

What is the process for leasing with Carfective?
At Carfective, you’re in full control of your car-buying journey. We take the stress out of the process by handling negotiations with the dealer on your behalf. Simply choose your ideal car, and we’ll negotiate on your behalf in alignment with your budgeted monthly payment and upfront costs.
What is the process of working with Carfective?
Your experience can be as hands-on or hands-off as you prefer. Carfective representatives are here to support you as much—or as little—as you need. If you just want to build your car and select your payment plan, we’ll take care of the credit check, approval, and delivery. Need more guidance? We’re happy to walk you through every step, from choosing the right packages to finding the best monthly payment. We’re here to make it easy.
Where can I make my lease payments?
All payments will be made directly to the financial institution that approved your lease. You’ll usually receive a statement by mail before your first payment is due, along with instructions for setting up your online account.
How long will it take for my lease to be approved?
Approval times vary based on your credit profile and chosen vehicle, as several factors come into play. In most cases, we receive a response within a few hours, though it can sometimes take 24–48 hours.
Who provides financing for my lease?
Carfective is not a leasing or financing provider; your lease agreement will be directly with the appropriate financial institution (e.g., Toyota Financial, BMW Financial, etc.).
How does Carfective get the best deals?
Carfective leverages deep market knowledge, exclusive dealer access, and real-time pricing to secure vehicles, discounts, and financing. We negotiate with top-rated dealerships, time purchases strategically, and eliminate unnecessary fees to deliver the best deals in a smooth, hassle-free experience.
What are the benefits of working with Carfective?
Carfective partners exclusively with top-tier dealerships to help you find the perfect car at the best price, with complete transparency at every step. We handle the legwork so you avoid sales pressure, streamlining the signing process.
What do upfront fees consist of?
Taxes, Title & Registration fees, Dealer Fees (documentation, processing, electronic filing fee), and Acquisition Fees (also known as bank fee or lease origination)

Down payment and 1st month payment may exist depending on the structure of the deal
